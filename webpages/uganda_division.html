Uganda Division

The Uganda division of Think Tree is dedicated to environmental sustainability by planting local tree varieties, promoting climate action through initiatives like tree planting with local communities, including primary school children, and with the hope of collaborating with other environmental conservation organisations to support reforestation efforts.
